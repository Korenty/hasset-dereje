Maternal Health & Girls’ Education

Mission

Her advocacy focuses on strengthening maternal health awareness in rural communities and ensuring girls have uninterrupted access to education.

Impact Areas

  • Community-based prenatal education sessions
  • Health awareness outreach programs
  • Girls’ school continuity initiatives
  • Local partnership mobilization

Vision

A future where no mother is left without care, and no girl is denied education due to circumstance. This is impact rooted in dignity.
