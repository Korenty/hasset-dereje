The Biography

Intellect Meets Grace.

“Reframing the global narrative of Ethiopia through the lens of excellence.”

Chemical Engineer • Miss World Africa • Humanitarian

Born in the heart of Addis Ababa, Hasset Dereje Admassu is a 20-year-old visionary whose journey transcends the traditional boundaries of pageantry. As a second-year Chemical Engineering student at Addis Ababa Science and Technology University, she balances the rigors of scientific analysis with the poise of a global icon.


In 2025, she made history at the 72nd Miss World Festival in India, becoming the Miss World 1st Runner-Up and Miss World Africa. Her mission is clear: to prove that African women are not defined by singular labels, but are multifaceted leaders capable of driving progress in STEM, diplomacy, and culture.

Education AASTU Chemical Engineering
Languages Amharic, English
Platform Beauty with Purpose
Hometown Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

The Vision

To see an Africa recognized not for its struggles, but for its resilience, its technological potential, and its cultural depth. Hasset is committed to being the bridge between traditional values and future innovations.
