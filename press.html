Official Biography & Media Resources

Short Biography

Hasset Dereje Admassu is Miss World Africa 2025 and 1st Runner-Up at the 72nd Miss World Festival. She is a Chemical Engineering student and advocate for maternal health and girls’ education.

Extended Biography

Born in Addis Ababa, Hasset represents a generation redefining Africa’s narrative — rooted in intellect, resilience, and global leadership. Her Beauty With a Purpose initiative supports maternal health awareness and rural girls’ educational access.
